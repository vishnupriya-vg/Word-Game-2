word game

I want you to create a word game that can be played by kids from grdes 3 to 8. The difficulty level of the words should be appropriate to the chosen grade. The words should not repeat across levels or grades. 
This is how the game should be- Odd One Out — "Bomb Tap"
 4 words appear as crates. One is the odd one out — it's a dud. Player taps it before a timer explodes all crates. Tap wrong = boom, round lost.
